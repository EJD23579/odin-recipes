Pizza Recipe

Description

This dish is one of the most widely known from the country of Italy , and it is a favourite of many people around the world. The recipe is created using flour, tomato sauce/paste and cheese, with some herbs as seasoning. However there are many variants and addtional ingredients you can add. Bon appetit!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato paste
  • Mixed Herbs

    • Steps

    1. Mix the flour with water and butter
    2. Roll out with a rolling pin until pizza shaped
    3. Add a layer of tomato paste to the base
    4. Add Cheese to yout satisfaction
    5. Add Herbs to your satisfaction
    6. Add any other ingredients such as sausage, pepparami, NOT PINEAPPLE
    7. Cook in the oven at 200 degrees celsius for 20 minutes
    8. Remove from oven and enjoy!
