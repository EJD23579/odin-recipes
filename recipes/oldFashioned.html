Old Fashioned Recipe

Description

A delicious, Whiskey based cocktail that was made popular in the USA. God Bless America

Ingredients

  • Whiskey/Bouron
  • Demarrera Sugar
  • One Orange
  • Soda Water
  • Angoustora Bitters

    • Steps

    1. Add 25ml of your chosen Whiskey or Bourbon to a glass
    2. Add two teaspoons of Demarrera Sugar to the Whiskey
    3. Stir until the sugar has dissolved
    4. Squeeze half an Orange into the glass and continue stirring
    5. Add a dash of soda water
    6. Add a dash of bitters
