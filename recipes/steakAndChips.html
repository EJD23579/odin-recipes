Steak and Chips Recipe

Description

This dish is one a classic in pubs all over the UK as well as other countries such as Brasil and America .

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Chips
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • Steps

    1. Preheat Oven to 200 degrees celsius and add chips for 25 minutes
    2. Put Butter into pan and heat until melted
    3. Add Steak to pan and cook until both sides are brown
    4. Remove Steak from pan and cut into it to check how cooked it is
    5. Replace Steak in pan if wanted
    6. When Steak is cooked to your taste remove from pan and season with Salt and Pepper to suit
